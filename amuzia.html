Amazing Race

This is a competitive treasure hunt designed for the tacticians and strategists. It involves a series of clues which lead the participant(s) from one location to another. Quick thinking and problem solving abilities are required in this event. The teams must compete to be the fastest by solving the clues and finding next locations.

Bidzkreig

This event will test the analytical skills of participants to test and select 11 players (also selecting corresponding players to have good chemistry between players) to create a football team. A limited amount of virtual money will be given to each team for placing bids. Each player will have specific points attributed to his name depending on his ratings in fifa and also on how good his chemistry is with other players of the same team/league/country.

Flash

Photography is an art of observation. It has little to do with the things one sees and everything to do with the way they see it. Do you think you have the sight of keen observation? If yes, surprise us with your images by participating in the categories that suit you the best. No restrictions on the number of categories you want to participate in!
